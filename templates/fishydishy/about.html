About Us


Fish are among the healthiest foods on the planet, with health professionals and medical bodies recommending we eat them at least twice a week to avail of their powerful vitamins and nutrients.

But while our local markets are abound with those nutritious and delicious fruits of the sea, most of us can’t tell our tunas from our trouts or our cod from our coley. And in a world where more and more people are eating cleaner and living greener, many other questions come to mind, ‘Which fish are sustainable?’, ‘Which are local to me?’ and even ‘How best to cook them?!’.

That’s where Fishy Dishy comes in.

Your one-stop hub for everything fish, Fishy Dishy allows users to browse the world’s fish by type and sustainability, read about where they’re from, their taste, texture and nutrition and check out our ‘Catch of the Day’ for the season’s most lauded fish.

Still struggling with getting a fish on your dish? Get acquainted with your local fishmongers with our handy map, and browse our user-uploaded recipes for some mouth-watering inspiration!

Got a great recipe you’d like to share? Upload it and let others try them, rate them and comment on them - the most popular recipes will be featured on our homepage every month. Fish tacos anyone?
